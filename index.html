R’nalytics Club

Data science is the trend of the next-generation technology, the dream for opening the entrance to the next era, and the difference to make on all aspects of future businesses and personal lives.

We make the idea concrete, real, and feasible, through building a forum to foster mutual learning and knowledge combination, developing a platform to expand the network of resources to create academic as well as industry opportunities, and forming cross-disciplinary teams to work on real-life big data projects and execute data-driven business plans.

We welcome graduate students in all majors and data science enthusiasts to join us to take the trend, realize the dream, and make a difference.
